# MindSpeak – AI-Powered Mental Health Platform

MindSpeak is a comprehensive **AI-powered mental health platform** designed to provide insights and practical tools for mental health professionals. It combines **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, **deep learning**, and **full-stack web development** to help doctors and therapists assess patients, track emotional trends, and maintain secure, structured communication.

---

## Features

### AI & Analytics
- Sentiment analysis and emotion detection  
- Six specialized BiLSTM + Attention models:
  - Depression classification  
  - Suicide risk assessment  
  - Anxiety and stress indicators  
  - Other mental health condition classifications  
- Multi-model inference pipeline  
- Emotional trend tracking and visualization  

### User Experience
- AI-powered chatbot for mental health support  
- Personalized dashboards with historical insights  
- Structured DSM-5 assessments (weekly updates)  
- Secure doctor–patient communication  

### Professional Services
- Doctor and therapist profile management  
- Appointment booking and scheduling system  
- Secure patient–professional workflows  

### Content & Engagement
- Mental health blog and article management  
- Educational content with admin-controlled publishing
