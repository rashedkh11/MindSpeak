# MindSpeak – AI-Powered Mental Health Platform

MindSpeak is a comprehensive AI-driven platform for mental health professionals. It integrates **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, **deep learning**, and full-stack web development to provide actionable insights, dashboards, and tools for patient assessment, emotional trend tracking, and secure doctor–patient communication.

---

## Features

### AI & Analytics
- NLP-based sentiment and emotion analysis  
- Six deep learning models for mental health classification:
  - Sentiment analysis  
  - Emotion detection  
  - Depression classification  
  - Suicide risk assessment  
  - Anxiety & stress indicators  
  - Additional mental health conditions  
- Multi-model BiLSTM + Attention inference pipeline  
- Dashboard visualization for emotional trends and probabilities  

### User Experience
- AI-powered mental health chatbot  
- Personalized dashboards with historical insights  
- Weekly DSM-5–aligned psychological assessments  
- Secure doctor–patient communication  

### Professional Services
- Doctor and therapist profile management  
- Appointment booking and scheduling  
- Secure patient-professional workflows  

### Content & Engagement
- Mental health blog and article management system  
- Admin-controlled educational content publishing  

---

## How It Works
1. **Weekly Assessments:** Users complete DSM-5–aligned assessments for continuous monitoring.  
2. **AI Text Analysis:** Text from chatbot conversations and direct patient chats is preprocessed, tokenized, and analyzed by six BiLSTM + Attention models.  
3. **Data Aggregation & Visualization:** Outputs from assessments and NLP models are securely stored and displayed in dashboards for trend tracking and risk monitoring.  

---

## System Architecture
- Full-stack web application: **PHP, Flask, MySQL**  
- RESTful APIs for AI inference  
- Secure authentication with role-based access control  
- Modular, scalable, production-ready design
